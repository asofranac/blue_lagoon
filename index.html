Accommodations

Our accommodations include, beachfront doubles, that lead right out onto your own private section of Beach. Beachfront Suites with a balcony that overlooks the beautiful coastline, of the Caribbean Sea. Also, our top-level, or presidential Suites. Which are also, as roomy and luxurious as our Bridal Suites.

Amenities

Other amenities include; A banquet hall, our world-class Island restaurant, and for nightlife, our Blue Lagoon room. And please, don't forget our indoor and outdoor pool areas, for our guests, to meet and socialize. Our Resort is also available, for private functions, and wedding events. Get in touch with us. We are sure we can make your Wedding, a memorable event for you, and your guests.

Activities

We include boating, windsurfing, snorkeling, and also skin diving lessons in the waters surrounding our Islands. We have for your convenience, a workout gym, early morning yoga classes, and a driving range. For group activities we include jogging, and a hiking trail, with accommodations for beach volleyball, and a basketball court.
