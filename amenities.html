Amenities

Luxurious Spa

Massage is one of the healthiest things we can do for ourselves. It rejuvenates our mind body and soul. Here at Blue Lagoon, we offer our Signature Custom Massage, Deep tissue massage, Aromatherapy, Adirondack stone massage, and our Reiki Energy treatment.

Your therapist will use healing techniques, to deeply relax and center your body. While heightening your awareness, reducing pain and tension while bringing inner peace, and emotional resolution. We also offer couples treatment, and in room therapy.

World Class Golf

We offer a world-class nine-hole course that opened in 2012. We maintain this challenging course meticulously, which helps to, keeps it ranked among the top 20 best public golf courses in the area. The course features beautiful green park-like areas, and our signature island green, on the 9th hole.

Expect to be treated like royalty when you arrive for your tee time. And enjoy VIP service while you're on the course. Weather you are an, experienced, or inexperienced golfer, Blue Lagoon Golf Course is the one especially for you. It features multiple team areas to accommodate all skill levels.

Relaxing Pool

Relaxation pools, are a mixture of mineral water mix and are heated to 32 C. They have been designed for relaxation, and are also a great place for water exercise. Our relaxation pools have a convenient access ramp, sitting along the Eastern side, and a lounge Zone beside the entry stairs.

We do offer, our guests our, Overlook Bungalow's, with a plunge pool, that takes you right from your ocean entrance, to your freshwater dip pool, right outside of your room. Perfect for a relaxing rinse, after a snorkel session or sunset soak, complete with champagne.

5-star Restaurant & Bar

When you join us in our world famous Island restaurant, you can expect the finest ingredients, food that is traditional yet modern. You're sitting down to more than just a meal, you're in for unexpected culinary experience. Your sommelier will guide you through the wine pairings, like an old friend showing you around their hometown.

And let's not forget, our world-class Blue Lagoon room Bar and Lounge. A place where guests can get together, socialize, and relax . It's the people who are the real shining stars at this Resort.
