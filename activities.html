Activities

Snorkling

World-class reef provides a spectacular snorkling experience. See hundreds of varieties of fish, coral, as well as sea turtles, sharks, eels and dolphins.

Paddleboarding

Enjoy a leisurely day paddleoboarding in the lagoon where you can spot birds, fish, frogs, and other native species. On calm days, paddle out in the ocean and get up close to dophins and other marine life.

Wind surfing

For the more adventurous, enjoy wind surfing right from our beach. Instructions provided for novices.

Beach volleyball

Three beach side courts with balls provided. Every week we organize teams and a tournament for a fun challenge. The winning team gets free drinks at the pool side bar.

Tennis

Six clay courts available for day or evening games. Bring your racket or borrow one from us.

Putting green

Practice your putting before heading to the island's world-class golf course. Or just enjoy putting around for the afternoon. Putters and balls provided.
